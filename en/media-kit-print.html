Manifesto

How this
started.

I spent twenty years in hospitality. Algarve, Dublin, Brazil. I was a hotel director with 80 rooms, a receptionist with a single room key in a guesthouse, and everything in between.

One summer day in 2019, a guest named Mr. Cole — from Manchester, visiting Portugal for the first time with his wife — walked up to the check-in desk and asked me the question that shifts a perspective: "Is there anywhere locals actually go? Not what's on Booking."

I opened the drawer. I took out the blue cardboard folder. The commissions folder — restaurants paying us 15% per referred guest, surf schools in the package because they had signed the contract, fishing boats on our map because someone had paid for the placement.

I handed the folder to Mr. Cole and said: "We have these suggestions."

The next morning I crossed paths with him in the corridor. They had been to one of the folder restaurants. He said it was fine. But there was something in his tone — that polite resignation of tourists who sense they have been channelled, not recommended.

I went to the office, closed the door, and stared at the blue folder for a long time.

Portugal Travel Hub was born from the refusal to be another blue folder. We don't list businesses because they pay for position. We verify businesses because they meet editorial standards.

Ricardo Barbosa, Founder · 20 years in hospitality · Algarve, Dublin, Brazil
"There are two Portugals — the one that shows up on the booking platforms, and the one that actually exists."
Business model

Why we don't auction position.

The industry model
Booking · Expedia · TripAdvisor
  • Position determined by who pays most — pay-to-rank applied to every search
  • 15–25% commission per booking, deducted permanently
  • Mandatory boost fees to escape pages 3 and 4
  • The same 10 results across every region, every search
The PTH model
Portugal Travel Hub
  • Editorial verification, not paid visibility — selection is independent
  • No auctions, no bidding, no commission per booking
  • "Verified Partner" editorial seal — qualifies, does not buy position
  • Fixed monthly partnership — full predictability and transparency
