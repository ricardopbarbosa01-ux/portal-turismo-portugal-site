What is Portugal Travel Hub

An editorial portal with commercial purpose

Portugal Travel Hub is not a search engine or a marketplace. It is a reference platform combining three pillars to create the ideal partnership context.

Premium Editorial

Verified editorial content about beaches, surf, fishing and coastal experiences. No clickbait, no disguised sponsored lists. Every page reflects editorial quality criteria that build audience trust.

Real-Time Data

Surf conditions, live webcams, tides, wind and water temperature. The portal is a decision tool — not just inspiration. Users return regularly to check conditions before heading out.

Intent Capture

The personalised planning form captures travel intent by region, experience and dates — before the booking decision. A direct channel to connect the qualified traveller with the right business, no intermediaries.

How we choose partners

Editorial criteria and quality standards

The portal's credibility depends on the quality of the partners we feature. Selection is manual — we do not accept every application.

Real and verifiable business

Active online presence — website, Google Maps or profile on a recognised platform. The business must exist and be independently discoverable.

Positive reviews (minimum 4.0)

Rating of 4.0 or above on relevant public platforms (Google, TripAdvisor). Authentic review history with no signs of manipulation.

Quality photographic material

At least one quality photo representative of the business and venue. The portal profile needs to show what the user will find.

Response within 48 hours

The partner commits to responding to requests or leads captured by the portal within 48 business hours. We do not manage expectations for users left without a response.

Service relevant to coastal tourism

Surf, fishing, coastal accommodation, seaside dining, watersports or nature. Businesses without a direct link to coastal tourism are not eligible.

We do not accept
Operators without a valid licence or permit
Businesses with unresolved complaint history
Services with no link to coastal or nature tourism
Profiles without minimum photographic material
Businesses that cannot guarantee a 48-hour response
Pure advertising campaigns without editorial context

This list protects the user experience and, consequently, the value of each partner's presence on the portal.
