About Portugal Travel Hub

An independent editorial platform for Portugal's coastline — built for travellers who plan with care, not for those who settle for any list.

Editorial independence Verified information No hidden advertising

Portugal Travel Hub is an independent editorial platform dedicated to Portugal's coastline — a rigorous, verifiable guide for people who want to know where they are actually going, not just where an algorithm sends them.

We research, curate and maintain information on beaches, surf conditions, fishing grounds, webcams and coastal destinations across the entire country — from the Algarve to the Minho, from the Alentejo coast to the Azores. Water quality data is sourced from APA (the Portuguese Environment Agency). Every partner featured on the platform passes a manual editorial review. Sponsored content, when it exists, is always clearly identified.

The platform exists for travellers who plan their trips with intention: people who want to know the real state of a beach before leaving home, who are looking for the best surf or fishing spots, and who prefer to support verified local businesses over anonymous listings. We are not a marketplace or a search engine — we are an editorial guide that takes responsibility for what it publishes.

Our editorial methodology and commercial transparency model are openly documented. Anyone can read how we select information, how we identify commercial content, and where the boundaries are that protect the quality of this platform — on the Editorial Methodology and Commercial Transparency pages.
