Atlantic Surf School

Surf · Bodyboard · Equipment Rental

Nazaré Beach, Silver Coast, Portugal

About the school

Atlantic Surf School has been operating at Nazaré Beach since 2014, training surfers of all levels on Portugal's most iconic coastline. With instructors certified by the Portuguese Surfing Federation and small class sizes (max. 6 students per instructor), we guarantee safe, personalised learning in a great atmosphere. All quality equipment is included — boards, wetsuits and access to the warm-up area. Open year-round, with high season from June to October.
